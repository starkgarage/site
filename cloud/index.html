We work with Amazon Web Services 
Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a comprehensive, evolving cloud computing platform provided by Amazon. It provides a mix of infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS) and packaged software as a service (SaaS) offerings.
AWS provides a comprehensive suite of services and solutions to run sophisticated and scalable applications to help achieve better business outcomes. Running your applications in the AWS Cloud can help you move faster, operate more securely, and save substantial costs; all while benefitting from the agility, scale and performance of the cloud.

Everithing your bussiness needs... and more! 
AWS provides infraestructure and services for every challenge your bussines can have. High Performance Computing, Serverless Computing, Internet of Things, Machine Learning, Mobile Services, Content Delivery, Business Applications, Backup & Restore and E-Commerce are some of the many services that the platform provides.

TAKE YOUR FIRST STEP TO THE CLOUDS

How does our free tier works?

Easy... Simple... Free.

You contact us

You contact us via Email, and you send us a document explaining in detail your business, your infrastructure and your needs. We will contact you as soon as possible to give you an estimated delivery date of the features included in our free layer. Keep in mind that as it is free, many customers will request this service and the execution times could be extensive.

We analyze your reality

We will analyze in detail the document you sent us, and if necessary we will contact you to answer any doubts that may arise, to achieve an architecture that perfectly suits your needs. Keep in mind that delays in the evacuation of these doubts can affect the estimated date.

We design the aws architecture that best fits.

We will prepare a basic architecture document, based on the needs of your business. Standing on our experience, we will aim for a flexible, resilient and cost optimized architecture.
In addition, we will include descriptive information about the services used in the proposed architecture and a set of recommendations for the next steps in the migration to the cloud.

We send you an architecture diagram and our recommendations

We will send you the documents as soon as we finish them, so that you can have a better vision to decide if you want to continue on your way to the cloud.
Again, since it's free, the time it takes to get the work done may not be the most performant, but we will do our best to have your basic architecture diagram as soon as possible!
